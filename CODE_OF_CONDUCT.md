# Code of Conduct

Datacave is an open and welcoming community. We expect all participants to be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or abusive comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any private communication related to the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They may take appropriate and fair corrective action, including temporary or permanent bans.

To report a violation, contact the project maintainers privately. Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and discreetly.
